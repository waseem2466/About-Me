Havelock City Commercial Project - Elevator & Escalator Systems

At Havelock City Commercial Project, I was extensively involved in the installation, commissioning, and ongoing maintenance of a sophisticated vertical transportation system. This comprehensive project included:

  • High Zone Elevators (8 units): Equipped with advanced hydraulic brake systems for enhanced safety and precision, operating at 7 meters per second (7 MS speed).
  • Low Zone Elevators (8 units): Designed for efficient vertical movement, operating at 4 meters per second (4 MS speed).
  • Mid-Rise Car Park Lifts (2 units): Schindler S5500 models, optimized for smooth and reliable parking access.
  • Shopping Mall Elevators (9 mid-rise units): Schindler S5500 models, contributing to seamless customer flow.
  • Service Lifts (2 units): Essential for operational support and logistics.
  • All main tower elevators (High and Low Zone) are advanced Schindler S7000 models, featuring cutting-edge destination control port technology and integrated lobby vision systems for optimized traffic management and user experience, leveraging AI-driven algorithms for predictive crowd flow and energy efficiency.
  • The shopping mall also features 21 Schindler 9300 escalators, ensuring efficient and comfortable vertical circulation for visitors.
  • Advanced Troubleshooting & Maintenance: Successfully diagnosed and rectified critical issues, including a hydraulic brake malfunction on a Schindler S7000 high-zone elevator. This involved identifying an oil leakage from the machine braking pump, traced to an O-ring problem, which was promptly replaced to restore full operational capacity. Additionally, proactively managed the replacement of the traveling cable for the high-zone lift, ensuring continuous, safe operation.
My role ensured operational efficiency, strict safety compliance, and effective troubleshooting of complex technical issues across these advanced systems, contributing to a seamless and intelligent urban mobility experience.
